*Know What I'm Saying?* is a small, independent publication. This policy explains what happens to your information when you read it.

## What this site collects today

The site itself does not currently run analytics or tracking software, and it does not require an account to read anything. Beyond the standard, anonymized server logs that any website host keeps to keep things running, nothing about your visit is collected or stored by us.

If that changes (for example, if a privacy-respecting analytics tool is added later to understand readership at a basic level), this policy will be updated first, and any such tool will be chosen to avoid identifying individual readers.

## Newsletter

If you sign up for the newsletter, your email address is used only to send you new essays, notes, and the occasional personal update. It is never sold, rented, or shared with advertisers. You can unsubscribe at any time, and asking us to delete your email from the list will always be honored.

## Share Your Story submissions

Story submissions are collected through Google Forms, which has its own privacy policy governing how it handles the data you enter. We only see what you submit; we use it solely to consider stories for adaptation into future essays, and any story we use is edited and anonymized first. Submitting a story is never a guarantee it will be published.

## WhatsApp Channel

The WhatsApp Channel runs on WhatsApp/Meta's platform and is governed by their own privacy policy. We don't have visibility into who follows the Channel beyond what WhatsApp itself shows us.

## What we don't do

We don't sell your personal data. We don't use your story submissions, email, or any other information for anything beyond what's described above. And we don't have a marketing or advertising network embedded in this site.

## Changes to this policy

As the site adds features (analytics, products, a chosen newsletter provider), this page will be updated to reflect exactly what's in use, with the date below updated accordingly.

## Questions

If you have a question about your privacy here, write to hello@knowwhatimsaying.com.

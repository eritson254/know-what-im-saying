## Right now

*Know What I'm Saying?* currently has no sponsorships, no affiliate relationships, and no paid placements. Nothing on the site today earns a commission or fee from a third party.

## If that changes

Future digital products (guides, workbooks, or other resources) are planned but not yet launched. When they are, they'll be clearly labeled as products of this publication, with plain language about cost and what you're getting. If any external link ever becomes an affiliate link, that will be disclosed at the point where the link appears, not buried in this page alone.

## What we won't do

In line with this publication's [editorial policy](/editorial-policy), any future product or recommendation will be presented honestly. We won't promise that a guide, workbook, or any piece of writing will heal, fix, or transform your situation, and we won't use emotional distress as a reason to push a sale.

## Questions

If you ever come across something on this site that looks like it should have been disclosed and wasn't, tell us at hello@knowwhatimsaying.com.

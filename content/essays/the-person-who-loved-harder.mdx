There is a particular kind of loneliness that lives inside a relationship that looks, from the outside, like it's going fine.

She noticed it first in the small math she kept doing without meaning to — who texted first, who asked the better questions, who remembered the small things. The arithmetic never came out even, but for a long time she told herself that wasn't the point.

## What This Story Is Really About

This is not really a story about him. It's a story about the quiet decision to keep investing in a connection that has stopped asking anything of the other person — and the relief, even in heartbreak, of finally putting the calculator down.
